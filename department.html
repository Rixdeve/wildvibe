The Department of Wildlife Conservation (DWC)


The Department of Wildlife Conservation (DWC) is the principle government institution responsible for the protection of wildlife resources of the country, over its entire land and sea territories. DWC also bears the legal authority to establish and manage the network of Wildlife Protected Areas (WLPAs) of the country, which at present exceeds a total area of 8500km2; 14% of Sri Lanka's land surface.
